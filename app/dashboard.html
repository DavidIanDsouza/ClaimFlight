How ClaimFlight Works
1
AI-Powered Extraction

Your airline email is processed by a large language model via OpenRouter. It extracts the airline, flight number, departure and arrival airports, delay duration, and disruption date in structured JSON format.

2
Regulation Engine

The ICAO prefix of your departure airport determines jurisdiction. CY* routes to Canada (APPR §19), European prefixes to EU Regulation 261/2004, and all others to US DOT 14 CFR 250.

3
Stay22 Hotel Integration

A live hotel search for your arrival city is embedded with your disruption date, providing immediate accommodation options with affiliate tracking.

4
Demand Letter PDF

A formally worded statutory demand letter is generated citing the exact regulation, entitlement amount, accommodation URL, and bank payment instructions.

5
Chexy Payment Ledger

A synthetic payment record is created with claim breakdown, routing and account numbers, and full dispute status tracking. Sandbox only — no real transfers.
